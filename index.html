Our Privacy Promise

Your photos stay on your device. Your data stays private. No tracking, no selling, no nonsense.

What Fisho Does

Fisho is an AI-powered fish identification app that helps you identify fish species from photos. We believe in privacy-first design and local processing whenever possible.

Information We Collect

Photos and Images

  • What: Photos you take or select for fish analysis
  • How: Through the app's camera or photo selection
  • Purpose: AI-powered fish species identification
  • Storage: Photos remain on your device only

User Corrections

  • What: Corrections you make to AI predictions (species name, size estimates)
  • How: When you use the "Correct Prediction" feature
  • Current Status: Stored locally on your device only
  • Future Plans: We may ask for permission to share anonymous correction data to improve AI accuracy
  • Your Choice: Any future data sharing would be completely optional

Technical Information

  • What: Basic app usage data, error logs, device type, iOS version
  • How: Automatically collected when you use the app
  • Purpose: Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Details: No personal identifiers, completely anonymous

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Your photos are not stored on our servers
  • Browsing history or other app usage
  • Contact lists or social media accounts
  • Any data from other apps on your device

How We Process Your Data

Local Processing First

  • Fish analysis happens on your device when possible
  • Photos may be temporarily sent to our AI service for complex analysis
  • Images sent for analysis are immediately deleted after processing
  • No photos are stored on our servers

Your Corrections

  • Currently: All corrections are stored only on your device
  • Future: We may ask permission to share anonymous correction data
  • No photos would ever be included in any data sharing
  • All shared data would be completely anonymous

Data Sharing Policy

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

Limited Sharing (Anonymous Only)

  • Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics may be shared with AI research partners
  • Technical support data may be shared with service providers under strict confidentiality
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

How We Protect Your Data

On Your Device

  • Photos remain in your device's photo library under iOS protection
  • App settings and corrections stored in secure local storage
  • No cloud backup of sensitive data

In Transit

  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3)
  • Photos sent for analysis are encrypted during transmission
  • Images are immediately deleted after processing

Data Retention

  • Photos: Never stored on our servers
  • Corrections: Stored locally on your device indefinitely
  • Technical logs: Kept for up to 1 year for debugging

Your Rights and Control

Photo Access

  • You can revoke photo library access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos
  • You can delete photos from your device at any time
  • App will continue to work with photos you explicitly select

Data Control

  • All correction data is stored locally - you have complete control
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us to request information about any server-side data
  • Right to request deletion of any data we may have

App Permissions

Fisho requests only essential permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of fish (optional)
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos (optional)

Children's Privacy

Fisho is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately for removal.

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app improvements or legal requirements
  • Changes will be posted within the app and on this page
  • Significant changes will require your consent
  • Continued use after minor changes constitutes acceptance

Third-Party Services

Fisho may use these third-party services under strict privacy agreements:

  • AI Processing: OpenAI or similar services for fish identification
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics only
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous error logs to fix bugs

All third-party services operate under strict data protection agreements and cannot access your personal information.

International Users

By using Fisho, users outside the United States consent to the transfer and processing of anonymous technical data in the United States, where our servers are located. No personal information or photos are transferred.
